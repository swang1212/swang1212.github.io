Medical Devices · R&D

Suture Attachment Verification — Implantable BCI

Led an end-to-end design verification test at Synchron evaluating the mechanical integrity of the suture attachment feature on an implantable brain-computer interface. Defined test parameters through engineering analysis and direct consultation with physicians and subject matter experts to model realistic in vivo loading conditions. Designed and 3D-printed custom test fixtures, executed mechanical testing on an Instron load frame, and authored the full verification report. Test passed and was submitted to the FDA.

Computational Research

Knee Implant Geometry Optimization

Conducted a parametric computational study in MATLAB to determine optimal geometry for a total knee implant. Varied femoral and tibial curvature and insert thickness across sagittal and frontal planes, generating parametric sweep visualizations of stress distributions. Identified geometry configurations that minimized contact stress and synthesized findings into clear engineering design recommendations.

Capstone · Hardware

Automated Multi-Stage Lego Sorting System

Designed and built a fully automated Lego sorting device as a capstone team project, inspired by industrial manufacturing techniques. A vibrating, multi-tiered tower passively segregates pieces by size via graduated apertures and tilt-driven chutes. A final-stage color sorter — built with a color sensor and servo motor — routes pieces into labeled buckets, creating a hands-free end-to-end sorting pipeline.
